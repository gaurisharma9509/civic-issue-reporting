Our Mission & Vision

🟣 Our Mission

Our mission is to create cleaner, safer, and smarter cities by giving people a simple way to report problems and get them solved quickly.

🟣 Our Vision

We want to build a smart city system where every citizen can actively participate in improving their surroundings using technology.

Simple Process

How It Works

Reporting civic issues should be fast, transparent, and simple for every citizen.

📸 Snap a Photo

Capture a clear photo of potholes, garbage, broken lights, water leaks, or any issue affecting your surroundings.

📍 Submit Report

Add issue details, pin the exact location, and submit the complaint directly to the concerned authorities.

✅ Track Progress

Stay updated with live progress notifications until the issue is resolved successfully.
